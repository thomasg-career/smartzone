Synapse '25

Synapse is the annual IoT exhibition organized by the Department of Computer Science at Kristu Jayanti University, powered by the SmartZone IoT Club.

It is a platform for students to showcase innovative projects that blend sensors, automation, and AI to solve real-world problems. Synapse brings together technology enthusiasts, encourages collaboration, and inspires new ideas for a smarter and more connected future.

What is IoT?

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a network of physical devices—sensors, appliances, vehicles, and machines—embedded with software and connectivity to collect, share, and act on data. IoT transforms how we live and work by enabling real-time insights and intelligent automation in homes, campuses, industries, and cities.
